We are Serverbee

A few interesting facts about Serverbee Team:

Serverbee has officially existed since 2019. During this time, we have implemented several dozen large projects with Kubernetes integration in such areas as IoT, E-commerce, Paas, blockchain, and many others, where we provide a range of services that include implementation, staff training, monitoring, consulting, etc.

Our employees have more than 7 years of experience in this field and are Certified Kubernetes Administrators (CKA) certified by The Linux Foundation and Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF).

We create, supervise and repair Cloud Infrastructure for the successful development of your business!

Our mission

The mission of Serverbee company is to take over the routine and responsibility of service support infrastructure so that the client's employees can devote time to more important things and develop their projects. Advantages of Serverbee: - we are a team of strong skills engineers, we are a remote-only distributed team around Europe, our expertise is focused on quality, and we use cutting-edge opensource tech for the best efficiency and wide functionality of our services.

  • We offer the client the optimal solution, justified by the right choice of technology for his service or project.

  • We also provide support to the client, training his staff to use most of the potential of the newest technology we have implemented.

  • We adhere to the approved action plan documented in the roadmap and adhere to its deadline(s).
